# ChainSight — AI Supply Chain Insights

## Project Overview
ChainSight is an AI-assisted analytics system designed to analyze supply chain data to uncover demand patterns, operational bottlenecks, and inventory inefficiencies. The project processes transactional and logistics datasets to generate insights that help optimize inventory planning, distribution strategies, and overall supply chain performance.
________________________________________
## Key Questions

•	How can supply chain data be used to detect demand fluctuations and forecast trends?

•	Which stages of the supply chain experience the highest delays or inefficiencies?

•	How do inventory levels affect fulfillment rates and operational costs?

•	Which products or regions experience the most supply-demand imbalance?

•	How can AI-assisted analytics improve supply chain decision-making?
________________________________________
## Approach

•	Data Ingestion: Import supply chain datasets containing order records, inventory levels, shipment data, and supplier information.

•	Data Cleaning: Standardize product identifiers, handle missing shipment data, and normalize transactional records.

•	Feature Engineering: Generate metrics such as inventory turnover, demand variability, and order fulfillment rates.

•	AI-Assisted Analysis: Apply predictive models and analytical techniques to detect demand trends and operational bottlenecks.

•	Visualization: Build dashboards to track supply chain performance, inventory distribution, and logistics efficiency.
________________________________________
## Insights & Impact

•	Identified demand fluctuations across products and regions.

•	Highlighted supply chain stages contributing to delivery delays.
